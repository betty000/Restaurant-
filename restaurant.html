About Us

Wangari Bar & Restaurant brings authentic Ethiopian cuisine and hospitality to Ambo in Ethiopia's West Shewa Zone. We celebrate Ethiopia's rich culinary heritage with modern elegance in this attractive setting.

Our menu features traditional dishes like Kikel, Tibse, and Tere sega , alongside innovative cocktails inspired by Ethiopian flavors. We source the finest ingredients to ensure every meal is a memorable experience.

Join us for an unforgettable journey through Ethiopian gastronomy in a warm and welcoming atmosphere at Ambo's premier attractive restaurant.
