About Us

Wangari Bar & Restaurant brings authentic Kenyan cuisine and hospitality to Nairobi. Named after Wangari Maathai, we celebrate Kenya's rich culinary heritage with modern elegance.

Our menu features traditional dishes like Nyama Choma, Ugali, and Samosas, alongside innovative cocktails inspired by Kenyan flavors. We source the finest ingredients to ensure every meal is a memorable experience.

Join us for an unforgettable journey through Kenyan gastronomy in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
